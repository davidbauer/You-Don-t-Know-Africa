Based on 20035 plays at youdontknowafrica.com/3, from players all over the world. These numbers are obviously not representative for a general public, mainly because people who self-select to play such a game are probably more likely to know a good amount of African flags. In the game, flags guessed incorrectly will be asked again later in the game, which means the same player can add up multiple wrong guesses (and eventually a correct guess) for the same flag.
